Ethos & Ethics

We are a small team of experts in HPC, cloud and distributed systems. We have a wealth of experience in the financial service and engineering.

We’re not trying to maximise shareholder value. We’re not chasing our next private equity funding round either. Our goal, plain and simple, is to do the right thing.

  • The right thing by our clients.
  • The right thing by our employees.
  • The right thing by our suppliers.
  • The right thing by our partners.

This isn’t easy. It means have chosen to remain completely independent. We are not aligned with any cloud provider. Instead, we work with them all. We’re not partnered or tied to a technology stack, vendor or company.

In doing so, we can be confident that our interests truly align with those of our clients. We’re not here to try and sell you the latest product or technology buzzword. We aren’t constrained in our choice of recommended solutions. Or words. Or Opinions.

We’re here to help you build the best possible system. HPC or single node. Distributed or not.

Hamza Mian
